Instructions

Scroll down the page to find West, Midwest, East, and South brackets. Each team's button has two parts, the first is the name of the team, and the number in the parenthesis is their seed. A bigger seed number means that they are ranked lower (less likely to win). To advance a team to the next round, you click on the button with their name and seed. When you click on the team that advances past the region's finals, they show up as the winner of the region and automatically get moved to the Final Four bracket at the bottom of this page. If you accidentally click the wrong button for a game, you can just click the other team's button but ensure that all subsequent games do not have the eliminated team. If you would like to start the bracket over, there are also Restart buttons at the top of every region's bracket. Note that hitting the Restart button will erase all your entries in EVERY bracket. At the bottom of the page you can predict the total number of points earned in the championship game. Once you are done filling out your choices, double check them, and fill your full name out at the bottom and hit the Submit button. Each student may only submit one bracket. We will ignore any submissions after your first in scoring.

The number of points you receive for predicting the winner corresponds to the round number (e.g. round 1 winners are worth 1 point, round 2 winners are worth 2 points, round 3 winners are worth 3 points, and so on). If you correctly predict that there will be an upset (the team with a lower seed wins), the number of points you receive for that game doubles (e.g. if you predict in round 3 that the lower seeded team in a game will win and they do win, you will get 6 points for that game). If two people get the same score, the prediction for the total number of points in the championship game will serve as a tiebreaker.
